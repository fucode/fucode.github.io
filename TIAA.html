Modernizing TIAA’s Market on Demand (MOD) Platform

As part of a long-term, multi-PI initiative at TIAA, I played a key role in modernizing the Market on Demand (MOD) platform, which included revamping a high-traffic fund screener tool and over 9,000 individual investment profile pages. The effort involved migrating to a React-based architecture to enhance performance, user experience, and maintainability.

  • Situation: The legacy MOD platform was outdated, slow, and difficult to scale. With over 9,000+ fund profile pages and a critical screener tool, TIAA needed a modern, responsive, and user-centric experience for both retail and institutional investors.
  • Task: Collaborate with internal teams and cross-functional stakeholders to lead the modernization effort of the MOD platform, including redesigning UI/UX, introducing new features, and implementing a React front-end to significantly improve load times and usability.
  • Action:
    • Gathered business and technical requirements through collaboration with Product, Design, Compliance, Accessibility, and Engineering teams across multiple PIs.
    • Led the definition and grooming of epics and user stories to align roadmap goals with delivery teams following Agile and SAFe frameworks.
    • Helped implement a scalable React-based front-end architecture that supported reusable components, improved responsiveness, and dramatically reduced page load times.
    • Launched new interactive features including a Hypothetical $10K Growth Chart and dynamic fund comparison tools to enhance investor decision-making.
    • Conducted regular demos and usability reviews with stakeholders to iterate on design and functionality based on feedback.
    • Partnered closely with compliance and accessibility teams to ensure WCAG standards and FINRA regulations were met throughout development.
  • Result: Successfully delivered a modern, high-performance MOD platform with improved UX/UI, responsive design, and new investor tools. Load times improved by over 60%, profile engagement increased, and the platform became easier to maintain and scale across teams.

PlanFocus 2.0: Modernizing TIAA’s Institutional Retirement Management Platform

I played a key role in the multi-year transformation of TIAA’s PlanFocus platform—our flagship institutional retirement plan management solution. The PlanFocus 2.0 initiative was a ground-up redesign, involving extensive collaboration across business, design, and engineering to deliver an intuitive, modern experience for plan sponsors and institutional partners.

  • Situation: PlanFocus was a critical yet outdated portal for retirement plan sponsors managing employee benefits. The platform lacked modern UI/UX, was not fully responsive, and offered a fragmented user experience across workspaces.
  • Task: Partner with cross-functional teams to redesign the PlanFocus platform from the ground up—upgrading legacy workspaces, aligning on entitlement flows, and launching new modules to enhance plan oversight, compliance, and education.
  • Action:
    • Worked across product, engineering, UX, legal, and data teams to design an improved information architecture and component library for the platform.
    • Defined detailed user flows and entitlement logic to support role-based access across multiple institutions and sponsors.
    • Helped redesign and launch key workspace modules, including:
      • My Plans: A dashboard delivering visualizations and metrics for plan health.
      • For Your Attention: A compliance-oriented task list with alerts and key dates.
      • Plan Review: A data-driven module to assess participant readiness and compare peer benchmarks.
      • Report & Analyze: Aggregated views across multiple plans to simplify strategic reporting.
      • Resources for Employees: Curated content (videos, articles) to increase engagement and education.
      • Resources for Plan Sponsors: Tools and references for managing complex retirement plans.
      • Investment Insights: Timely market commentary from TIAA-CREF experts to aid in plan monitoring.
    • Contributed to Agile PI planning, refinement sessions, and roadmap alignment over multiple program increments (PIs).
    • Conducted user acceptance testing (UAT) coordination and served as the liaison between business and engineering during rollouts.
  • Result: Successfully delivered a modernized PlanFocus platform used by thousands of institutional clients. Improved task management, data clarity, and accessibility helped plan sponsors make more informed decisions, enhance compliance oversight, and improve employee engagement with retirement benefits.
