Job Duties & Responsiblities


  • Business Analyst/Product Designer (UI/UX)
  • Documented all necessary design documentation, including wireframes, business rules, and technical requirements in functional specifications documents (FSD) and UX guides based on business requirements documents (BRD).
  • Worked with Stakeholders, Product Team, SMEs, and BAs to better understand the business requirements or user stories at a high-level focusing on answering ‘what is the business solution’ as opposed to ‘how to achieve the business solution’.
  • Translated BRD requirements to FSD describing the intended behavior of a system enhancements or features including data, operations, input, output, and the properties of the system.
  • Designed wireframes, interactive lo-fi to hi-fi prototypes using the design system for Wells Fargo
  • Developed scenarios, use cases, and engaging closely with stakeholders to define and provide high-level business (BRD) and UX requirements
  • Conducted stakeholders interview, usability sessions, and gathered metrics to help improve design solutions
  • Collaborated with BA team to design, validate, and analyze Business Requirement Document (BRD) into Functional Specific Document (FSD)
  • Facilitated UI development, and worked with front-end developers to implement mockups and solutions
  • Worked with QA team to validate functionalities and business flows

  • Agile/Scrum/Kanban
  • Worked with the Agila Coach and Champion to help transition our team from Waterfall to Agile
  • Documented artifacts within Confluence and help troubleshoot and train end-users on Confluence and JIRA
  • Led a team managing 5 apps on how to proceed the transition from a waterfall SDLC to Kanban
  • Worked with Product Owners and Technology Leadership to help define and improve our JIRA Kanban Board and workflow
  • Worked with the team to refine the Product Backlog and set ground rules for continuous workflow improvement
  • Trained team members on how to utilize JIRA functionality
  • Regularly checking the Kanban board and making sure that no work item has been blocked for too long
