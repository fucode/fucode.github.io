Wells Fargo Corporate & Investment Banking (CIB)

Wells Fargo’s Corporate & Investment Banking (CIB) division provides a full suite of financial services and advisory solutions to corporate, government, and institutional clients worldwide. The group supports capital raising, risk management, financing, and investment strategies across a range of sectors.

  • Key areas include Investment Banking, Markets, Commercial Real Estate, Asset-Backed Finance, and Loan Syndications.
  • CIB is supported by product, technology, operations, and risk teams focused on delivering secure, scalable, and client-centric solutions.
  • Strategic initiatives include digital modernization, regulatory compliance, and data platform enhancements across the lending lifecycle.

Through its global capabilities and deep industry knowledge, CIB plays a vital role in supporting institutional client success and growth.
