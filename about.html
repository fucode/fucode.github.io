Bio

I am a User Happiness Architect or UX/UI Designer, with a passion for creating human-centered designs. I am a creative problem solver that thinks outside the box.

My background has been as a Business and Data Analyst working with cross-functional teams including IT, Product Mangement, Design, Operations, Strategy, and Marketing.

My design philosophy revolves around empathy and constant iterations. The number one skill to have as a designer for me is empathy. This is something that comes naturally to me in everything I do. I am always trying to make workflows and tasks as simple for the end-user as possible. Iteration is another component of my design philosophy, just like in life all designs require constant iterations in order to evolve and improve.
